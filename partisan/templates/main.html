The Partisan Project

The Partisan Project sets out to measure the prevalence of divisive sentiment across a wide variety of topics such as politics, healthcare, and economics using AI to detect what people are talking about and how they feel about what people are saying.

This is a non-partisan initiative that does not have any political affiliation whatsoever.

Politics

Lets try to determine what people are talking about with regards to the US 2020 election... Sentiment is an important measure of how divided people are on a particular topic. A statement that is highly negative for instance is genereally more divisive than a statement that is positive or neutral.
